About Us
To offer a global touch, maintaining Bangladeshi culture in living and work places.

Shanta Group started its journey in 1988, and has become one of the forerunners in the ready-made-garment(RMG) export, earning a reputation as an important vendor for some of the most reowned apparel brands of USA and Europe. Shanta Group is one of the key members of the STS Group - the promoter of the world class Apollo Hospitals Dhaka, International School Dhaka (ISD) the only International Baccalaureate (IB) School of the country, and the Delhi Public School, Dhaka.
